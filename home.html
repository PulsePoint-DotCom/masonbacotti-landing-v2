What's this all about

Built by a teenager.
Made for the internet.

Mason Bacotti is a high schooler in New York who got tired of waiting for someone else to build the tools he wished existed — so he started building them himself.

masonbacotti.com is home to his internet projects: apps that track what's about to go viral, tools that make learning feel like a game, and experiments that push the boundaries of what a student can build on the web.

No venture capital. No enterprise team. Just an 8th grader with a laptop, curiosity, and a willingness to ship. Everything here is built fast, deployed free, and made for real people — not investors.
