Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how masonbacotti.com ("we," "our," or "this site") handles information.

Short version: This is a static site. We don't collect your data. The only third-party services we use are Google AdSense (for ads) and Cloudflare (for hosting/security). Each has their own privacy policy.

Information We Collect

This site is a static informational website. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from visitors.

Third-Party Services

Some pages use third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — used for hosting, DNS, and security. Cloudflare Privacy Policy →
  • Google AdSense — used to display advertisements on some pages. Google uses cookies and device identifiers to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings →. How Google uses data from sites using its services →
  • Google Fonts — used to load fonts on this site. Google Privacy Policy →

Cookies

We do not use first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google AdSense, Cloudflare) may use their own cookies per their respective policies. Google AdSense may use cookies for ad targeting and measurement.

Advertising

Google AdSense displays advertisements on some pages of this site. Google may collect information about your visit to display relevant ads. This includes:

  • Information about your browser and device
  • Pages you visit on this site
  • Ads you clicked on before coming here

You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time: Google Ads Settings →

Links to Other Sites

This site contains links to external projects (e.g., pulsepoint.masonbacotti.com, mathdrill.masonbacotti.com, btwv.masonbacotti.com). Those sites have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Our projects are designed for general audiences.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising (use Google Ads Settings →)
  • Request deletion of any data Google has collected about you
  • Restrict how your data is used

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact
